The Science of Stress: A Neural Perspective

In the modern world, stress is more than a feeling—it's a physiological signature written in our brain waves. SynapseMonitor decodes this signature in real-time. By monitoring the subtle electrical shifts across the cerebral cortex, we can identify the transition from "Flow" to "Fight or Flight" before you even feel the tension.

Decoding the Neural Signal: Our Process

01

Signal Acquisition

Multi-channel EEG sensors capture micro-voltage fluctuations from the frontal, parietal, temporal, and occipital lobes.

02

Fast Fourier Transform

Raw temporal waves are decomposed into their constituent frequencies (Delta, Theta, Alpha, Beta) using FFT algorithms.

03

Biometric Correlation

We analyze the ratio between high-beta (stress) and alpha (relaxation) waves to calculate your objective Stress Index.
