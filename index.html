Understanding EEG Frequency Bands

🌊 Delta Waves (0.5-4 Hz)

When: Deep sleep, unconscious state

Represents: Restorative sleep, healing, regeneration

High levels: Deep relaxation, sleep

Low levels: Poor sleep quality, brain injuries

💭 Theta Waves (4-8 Hz)

When: Light sleep, meditation, daydreaming

Represents: Creativity, intuition, memory

High levels: Deep relaxation, creativity, meditation

Low levels: Anxiety, stress, poor emotional awareness

🧘 Alpha Waves (8-13 Hz)

When: Relaxed wakefulness, calm focus

Represents: Restful alertness, calmness, reduced stress

High levels: Relaxation, daydreaming, brain idling

Low levels: Anxiety, high stress, insomnia, OCD

⚡ Beta Waves (13-30 Hz)

When: Active thinking, focus, problem-solving

Represents: Alert consciousness, concentration

High levels: Anxiety, high arousal, stress, paranoia

Low levels: Daydreaming, poor cognition, depression

🚀 Gamma Waves (30-100 Hz)

When: Peak concentration, high-level information processing

Represents: Learning, memory, information processing

High levels: Learning, peak focus, expanded consciousness

Low levels: Learning disabilities, memory problems, ADHD
